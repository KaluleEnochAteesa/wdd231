What are the Benefits of Using Traditional Herbal Remedies?

Traditional herbal remedies have been used for centuries to treat various ailments. Some of the benefits include natural healing properties, fewer side effects compared to synthetic drugs, and the ability to treat the root cause of illnesses rather than just the symptoms. Herbal remedies are often more affordable and accessible, making them a valuable option for many people.
